Gesture Guide

♫ Right Hand — Melody

1⃣1 finger = Sa (C)
2⃣2 fingers = Re (D)
3⃣3 fingers = Ga (E)
4⃣4 fingers = Ma (F)
5⃣5 fingers = Pa (G)
⇧Hand height = octave shift (low/mid/high)
↔Combos: idx+mid=Dha(A), mid+ring=Ni(B)

♬ Left Hand — Drums

1⃣Index = Tabla Dha (low)
2⃣Middle = Tabla Tin (high snap)
3⃣Ring = Mridangam Thom
4⃣Pinky = Kick drum
5⃣Thumb = Hi-hat / Kanjira snap
✶Combos: idx+mid=Clap, ring+pinky=Dhol boom, all=Full hit
